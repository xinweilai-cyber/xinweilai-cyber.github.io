新音乐 —— Erutan

發表於 2024-09-04

#新音乐

· 乐者简介 ·

Erutan

From USA

I grew up in a music studio. My mother was a private Classical violin teacher and taughtlessons in our home. There was never a moment where I wasn’t listening to themusic of her students or vinyl records of classical, ancient/medieval, world,and many other types of music on our old turntable.

I was presented with and started playing my first violin at age one and a half.It was a 1/32 size, which is the smallest practical size of violin available.Apparently, by all accounts, I took to violin playing like a “duck towater”, although for the first year I would not allow anyone to help me.Still, as I was turning two years of age, I had already figured out how to playmany of the songs and pieces my mom’s students played.

I was a child who actually enjoyed practicing—often 5 or 6 hours daily. Intime I went on to win several competitions, played solos in front of severalorchestras and gave many performances. Perhaps my proudest moment came when Iauditioned for and was accepted as a member of Disney’s Young Musician’ssymphony Orchestra (DYMSO) which held auditions in the USA, Canada, and theU.K. With that orchestra, I performed at the Hollywood Bowl and was able tomeet and perform with several celebrated musicians and actors (Mark o’Connor,Michael Bolton, Elmer Bernstein, Tom Hulce, and many others) It was a lovely,rewarding experience, and many of the other talented children in that orchestrahave gone on to become world famous competition winners, have been awardedclassical recording contracts, or have performed on movie soundtracks.

我在音乐工作室中长大。我的母亲是一位私人古典小提琴教师，她在我们的家中教授课程。无论何时，我总是听着她学生演奏的音乐，或是在我们的老式唱机上播放的古典、古代/中世纪、世界音乐，以及其他各种类型的音乐。

我在一岁半时得到了我的第一把小提琴，那是一把1/32尺寸的小提琴，这是可用的最小实用尺寸的提琴。据说，当时所有人都觉得我对小提琴的热情如同“如鱼得水”，虽然在第一年里我不允许任何人帮助我。但就在我快两岁时，我已经学会了演奏许多我妈妈的学生们所演奏的曲目。

我是一个真正喜欢练习的孩子——每天经常练习5到6个小时。随着时间的推移，我赢得了几场比赛，在多个乐团面前独奏，并进行了许多演出。也许我最自豪的时刻是当我参加迪士尼青年音乐家交响乐团（DYMSO）的试演并被录取的时候，该乐团在美国、加拿大和英国举行试演。与那个乐团一起，我在好莱坞碗剧场演出，并且有机会与几位著名音乐家和演员（如马克·奥康纳、迈克尔·波顿、埃尔默·伯恩斯坦、汤姆·休斯等）见面并一起演出。这是一次美好且有意义的经历，那个乐团中的许多其他才华横溢的孩子后来都成为了世界著名的比赛冠军，获得了古典音乐的录音合约，或是在电影配乐中演出。
